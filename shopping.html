Shopping in Madison

Where to Browse & Buy

From local makers’ markets to State Street boutiques and larger centers like Hilldale, Madison has a mix of unique finds and everyday essentials. Here are a few places I like to stop by.

Top Spots

  1. State Street: Books, UW gear, thrifts, and gifts all in one stroll.
  2. Hilldale: Boutiques, home goods, and weekend pop-ups.
  3. Dane County Farmers’ Market: Produce plus artisan foods and crafts around the Capitol.
  4. Willy Street: Vintage, local art, and neighborhood staples.
