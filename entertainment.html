Entertainment in Madison

What to Do for Fun

Madison’s entertainment scene blends live music, local theater, sports, and nightlife. From catching a show on State Street to cheering at Camp Randall, there’s always something going on no matter the season.

Popular Spots & Events

  1. Orpheum Theater: Historic venue hosting concerts, comedians, and film festivals.
  2. Majestic Theatre: Smaller space for indie bands and dance nights downtown.
  3. Camp Randall Stadium: Badger football, tailgates, and electric fall Saturdays.
  4. Barrymore Theatre: Local shows and community events on the east side.
  5. Monona Terrace: Seasonal rooftop events with skyline and lake views.
