Outdoor Activities Around Madison

Outdoor Must-Do's

Madison is surrounded by lakes, parks, and various trails that make it an amazing destination for outdoor activities. It's warm summer temperatures allows for all day fun out and about. Whether you elect to stay downtown or dive deep into nature, there are amazing options either way.

Top Outdoor Spots

  1. Memorial Union Terrace: Enjoy live music, colorful chairs, and lake views. The terrace is a Madison staple and is an awesome place to see the Madison community.
  2. UW Arboretum: Miles of walking trails and native Wisconsin habitats.
  3. Lake Monona Loop: A scenic 13-mile bike route circling the lake.
  4. Vilas Park & Zoo: Great for families and a short walk from the shoreline + its free!!
