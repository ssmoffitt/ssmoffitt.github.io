Dining in Madison

Food Culture

Madison’s food scene combines local farms, creative chefs, and a love for good company. Whether you want late-night cheese curds, rooftop brunch, or a fine-dining experience, the city has something for every taste and budget. As a student i've had the chance to try meals at all different price points. When i'm alone I normally am balling on a budget(as most students do), but when my parents come to town we really get aorund. 8 years of visiting Madison really builds your knowledge of the best food around town.

Top Local Eats

  1. Cassetta's: Hands down the best lunch in Madison. If you're a sucker for fresh baked bread, fresh ingredients, and sandwiches, this place is for you.
  2. Harvey House: Strong foothold as the best resturant in Madison. Chefs from the world renowned French Laundry.
  3. Ahan: Easily the strongest ethnic cuisine in Madison. Plan your trip to ahan for the best South East Asian food around.
  4. State Street Brats: Quintessential Wisconsin experience. Great burgers, brats, and cheese curds. Iconic patio and probably the best place to catch a game and have a beer.
